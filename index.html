International Freight

Import & Export Services

Integrated import and export logistics — from port pickup and container handling through to warehousing, destuffing, repacking, and final delivery. Engineered for enterprise-scale cargo operations with full compliance and real-time visibility.

Wharf Cartage

Full-spectrum import and export container movements from all major Australian ports. Our NHVR-compliant fleet of prime movers, sideloaders, and flat-tops covers 20ft and 40ft containers through to overweight, refrigerated, and specialised cargo — with GPS visibility at all times.

Container Destuff & Pack

Professional container destuffing and packing at our purpose-built facilities across all metro hubs. Cargo is unloaded, inspected, sorted, and palletised to specification. Full container repacking and export load consolidation available for outbound shipments of all types.

Port-to-Door Delivery

Seamless container pickup from terminal, cartage to our facilities for destuff or direct delivery, and final-mile distribution to warehouse, retail, or end-customer locations. Full freight visibility from port gate to delivery confirmation throughout.

Dangerous Goods Handling

Certified handling of dangerous goods and hazardous cargo in compliance with the Australian Dangerous Goods Code and all relevant state and federal regulations. Trained operators manage packaging, segregation, documentation, and transport for all DG classifications.

Cross-Docking & Consolidation

High-speed cross-docking at key metro nodes allows rapid transfers between transport modes without intermediate storage. LCL and FCL consolidation services reduce freight costs for clients managing multiple import or export consignments.

Cargo Reporting & Visibility

Real-time container tracking, customs milestone updates, and delivery confirmation integrated into a client-facing reporting dashboard. Full audit trails and proof-of-delivery documentation available for every movement across the network.
